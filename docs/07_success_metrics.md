## The Recruiter Test

### "Why would a recruiter stop scrolling?"

**Because the project title alone signals something different:**

> "Causal Demand Intelligence: A system that explains *why* demand changes, not just how much"

Compare to typical portfolios:
- ❌ "Demand Forecasting with ARIMA, Prophet, and XGBoost"
- ❌ "Sales Prediction using Machine Learning"
- ❌ "Time Series Analysis of Grocery Sales"
- ✅ "Causal Demand Intelligence with External Signal Detection"

### "What would the recruiter see in 30 seconds?"

1. **GitHub README** with architecture diagram, deployed app link, and clear problem statement.
2. **Live Streamlit app** where they can interact with the counterfactual simulator.
3. **Causal DAG visualization** — something they've never seen in a student portfolio.

### "What questions would this generate in an interview?"

- "How did you decide which variables to include in your causal graph?"
- "What was the most surprising causal effect you found?"
- "How does causal safety stock differ from traditional safety stock?"
- "Could this approach work with our data?"

These are *exactly* the questions you want — they let you demonstrate depth, business judgment, and technical skill simultaneously.

### What Does NOT Exist (or is extremely rare)

- Amazon: Agentic AI supply chain tools
